International Daiyi's Walking Day

 

Yi Dai has his own annually festival: International Daiyi's Walking Day.

During the Walking Day, Yi Dai hikes 40~50 km by normally 60000~70000 steps.

He sets off at 8~9 o'clock in the morning and reaches his planned destination at 17~18 o'clock.

In the rest of the day, He will have some 'free walking' to get more steps.

 

The Walking Day occurs every year between March and April.
The first Walking Day was in 2019 and that day Yi Dai walks around 40 km crossing Lanzhou city during his cozy QingMing holiday.
The date of the Walking Day is not fixed. It is dependent on the weather (usually between 10 and 20 degree Celsius), rainfall (no rain) and Yi Dai's physical and mental state.
