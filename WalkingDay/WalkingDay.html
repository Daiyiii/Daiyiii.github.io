International Daiyi's Walking Day

 

Yi Dai has his own annually festival: International Daiyi's Walking Day.

 

The Walking Day occurs every year between March and April.
The first Walking Day was in 2019, where Yi Dai walks across Lanzhou city from the east (Chengguan District) to the west (Xigu District) during his cozy QingMing holiday.
The date of the Walking Day is not fixed, which is dependent on the weather (usually between 10 and 20 degree Celsius), rainfall (no rain) and Yi Dai's physical and mental state.
